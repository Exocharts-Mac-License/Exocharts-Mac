## How to Install

1. Download the latest version of Exocharts for macOS.  
2. Open the DMG installer and move Exocharts.app to the Applications folder.  
3. Launch the app and allow it through macOS Gatekeeper if required.

## Overview

<a href="#">Exocharts for macOS</a> is built for traders who interpret markets primarily through order-flow and volume behavior rather than simple price lines. It provides footprint charts, volume profiles and heatmaps that expose the underlying structure of auctions as they form in real time. Instead of guessing where liquidity might appear, users can see how it is actually interacting with price. This perspective supports a more evidence-based approach to trading, where entries and exits are grounded in observable behavior.

The platform organizes charts and tools so that related instruments and timeframes can be monitored side by side. Futures traders can watch the relationship between different contracts, while spot traders can focus on the way high-volume nodes develop and decay. Adjustable aggregation, custom filters and <a href="#">delta-based views</a> let users tune the visualizations to match their reading style. Over time, patterns such as absorption, exhaustion and initiative activity become easier to recognize.

Exocharts on macOS also emphasizes smooth interaction and low-latency rendering. Panning, zooming and switching layouts remain responsive even when many charts are open, allowing traders to move quickly from broad overviews to precise microstructure inspection. Snapshot and replay tools help users study sessions after the fact, building a library of reference scenarios that inform future decisions. This is particularly valuable for traders who treat chart review as a core part of their practice.

The overall design aims to support long hours of focused observation. Colour choices, contrast levels and typography are tuned so that critical signals stand out without creating visual fatigue. Integration with macOS window management features makes it easy to assign Exocharts to specific desktops or monitor arrangements. As traders refine their workflows, the platform becomes a specialized lens onto the market, revealing details that are invisible in conventional price charts.

## Major Features
- Footprint charts revealing traded volume at each price level  
- Volume profile tools with adjustable aggregation settings  
- Heatmaps that visualize resting and shifting liquidity  
- Delta views that highlight aggressive buying and selling  
- Multi-chart layouts for correlated instruments and timeframes  
- Session replay and snapshot tools for structured review  
- Responsive chart rendering optimized for macOS desktops  
- Extensive customization of colours, scales and filters  
- Support for futures and other order-book driven markets  
- Workspace presets for different trading templates  
- Local caching for fast navigation between instruments  
- Designed to complement, not replace, core execution platforms
